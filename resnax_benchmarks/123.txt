// natural language
An identifier must begin with either an alphabet or underscore (""_""), it can not begin with a digit or any other special character, moreover, digits can come after.

// example
"gfg",+
"_abc12",+
"123",-
"#abc",-

// gt
and(startwith(or(<let>,<_>)),repeatatleast(or(<alphanum>,<_>),1))
